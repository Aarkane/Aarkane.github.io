RSA Project

The RSA project was an excersise to further our understanding of encryption and decryption. Skills we gained from this project are: generating encryption keys, an understanding of the chr and ord functions, and developing programs capable of simple encryption and decryption. The Procedure is as follows: firstly we paired up and calculated a unique encryption key. Then we posted our public key on a shared document for other pairs to see. The other pairs then encrypted a message using our key and posted it in the same document. Using our private key, we then decrypted the message and posted it below the encrypted version. Similarly, we used the other pair's public keys to send them encrypted messages. We were finished once we had sent messages to all the other pairs and decrypted all the messages we recieved.
